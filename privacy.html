Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

At Stackifie.online, a brand operated by Malwa Trucking LLC, we prioritize the security and privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website to search for and inquire about event tickets.

1. Information We Collect

We collect basic information required for order fulfillment and customer support, including your name, email address, phone number, and billing details. We do not store full credit card numbers on our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is strictly used to process transactions, deliver tickets, and provide customer service. We also use cookies to optimize our advertising campaigns on platforms like Google Ads and Microsoft Advertising (Bing Ads).

3. Google Ads & Cookies

We use Google Ads and Microsoft Advertising (Bing Ads) to reach potential customers. These platforms may use cookies and tracking technologies to show relevant ads based on your browsing behavior. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. We also use Google Analytics to understand website traffic and improve user experience.

4. Data Security

All data is processed using industry-standard SSL encryption. We do not sell your personal data to third-party marketing firms.

5. Secondary Marketplace Disclosure

Stackifie is a ticket aggregator. We are not affiliated with any primary ticket provider, venue, artist, or event organizer. We do not sell tickets directly. All ticket purchases are completed on Ticketmaster.com. Any data collected is solely for the purpose of helping users discover events.
