About Us

Stackifie is a ticket aggregator that helps you discover live events across the USA. We display events from Ticketmaster and redirect you to Ticketmaster.com for ticket purchases. Operated under the corporate umbrella of Streamernet Info Private Limited, we bring a data-driven, customer-first approach to event discovery.

Our Mission

Our goal is to provide fans access to top-tier sports, music concerts, and theater performances through the secondary resale market, even when primary channels are no longer available.

Important Disclosure

Stackifie is a ticket aggregator and is not affiliated with any artist, team, venue, or event organizer. We do not sell tickets directly. All ticket purchases are completed on Ticketmaster.com. Prices may be above face value. All applicable taxes and fees are disclosed by Ticketmaster before any payment is required.
